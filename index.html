Research Snapshot

My research interests connect three core goals: (1) improving predictive performance and robustness, (2) building explanations that users can trust, and (3) supporting real-world adoption through responsible AI practices.

Focus Areas

  • Explainable AI: Grad-CAM visual attribution, confidence-aware analysis, and model behavior interpretation.
  • Transformers: BERT-family models for text classification and TimeSformer-style modeling for video understanding.
  • Healthcare & Bioinformatics: biomedical data analysis workflows for disease insight discovery.
  • Information Quality: credibility modeling, misinformation detection, and transparent decision support.
