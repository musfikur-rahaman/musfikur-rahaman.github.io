Biography

I am a doctoral researcher specializing in trustworthy and explainable Artificial Intelligence. My work integrates machine learning, deep learning, multimodal modeling, and generative AI to address challenges in healthcare informatics, computer vision, and information quality.

My research emphasizes interpretability, robustness, deployment-readiness, and ethical AI design. I aim to develop hallucination-resistant, human-centered AI systems capable of integrating text, image, and video modalities for real-world decision support.

Research Interests

  • Deep Learning & Transformer Architectures
  • Computer Vision & Video Analytics
  • Bioinformatics & Genomic Data Science
  • Explainable & Trustworthy AI
  • Information Quality & Data Governance
