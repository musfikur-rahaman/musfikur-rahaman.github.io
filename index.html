About

I am a PhD student in Information Science at the University of Arkansas at Little Rock. My research focuses on Trustworthy & Explainable AI, Multimodal Learning, Medical AI, Computer Vision, and Transformer-based models for video understanding.

I design interpretable, robust, and deployment-ready AI systems for healthcare, information quality, and real-world decision support.

Selected Projects

  • NewsLogic (2025) – AI-powered fake news detection system using BERT ensemble and LLaMA-based explanation engine.
  • Image Forgery Detection (2024–2025) – ResNet50 + U-Net segmentation with Grad-CAM explainability (AUC: 0.86).
  • Soccer Action Recognition (2025) – TimeSformer-based classification of 10 actions achieving 91.13% accuracy.
  • MLBioIGE – Machine learning + bioinformatics integration for SARS-CoV-2 pulmonary fibrosis analysis.
